Welcome to Kamla Foundation Website
Helping people in India

Kamla Foundation works in India with
people who are on the margins of society;
the poor, low-caste, tribal people, especially women -
to help them build and sustain their individual,
family and community livelihoods.
Our Vision is an India, where all people
have an opportunity to live in dignity,
free from hunger, deprivation and marginalisation
